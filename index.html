Who Are We?

BudayaBaik is a non-profit organization based in Indonesia, focused on creating sustainable environmental and social change. Our name translates to "Good Culture," which reflects our mission to promote positive practices that benefit both people and the planet.

Our Vision

A world where communities thrive in harmony with nature, preserving Indonesia's rich cultural and natural heritage for future generations.

Our Mission

To reduce environmental pollution, empower local communities through education and sustainable initiatives, and create lasting positive impact across Indonesia.

Recent Projects

Coastal Cleanup Initiative

Organized beach cleanups across Bali and Java, removing over 500kg of plastic waste from coastal areas.

Eco-Education Program

Environmental education workshops for children in rural communities, reaching 300+ students.

Community Recycling Center

Established a recycling facility in Yogyakarta that processes 200kg of waste weekly.

Our Impact

Since our founding in 2018, BudayaBaik has made significant progress in addressing environmental issues and supporting communities across Indonesia. Our work has reached 12 cities and villages, creating lasting change through sustainable initiatives.
