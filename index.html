Who Are We?

BudayaBaik is a non-profit organization based in Indonesia, est. in 2020, founded by NHJS students. We are a non-profit born from the belief that every child deserves a safe & sustainable place to learn. With a current mission to transform plastic waste into durable, eco-friendly classrooms, turning environmental challenges into educational opportunities. And pushing barriers of sustainability and quality education in Indonesia's underprivileged communities. Our name translates to "Kind Culture," which reflects our mission to promote positive practices that benefit both people and the planet.

Our 2025 Annual Goals

By 2025, Budayabaik aims to grow into a non-profit of 50+ youth changemaker and dedicate ourselves to remove 2 tonnes of plastic waste from Indonesia's overflowing landfills and transform it into eco-blocks to finish the rebuilding of sekolah PAUD in Bogor, Indonesia. We aim to empower 580+ local underprivileged community members through waste management and environmental & green education initiatives. And promote quality education through providing semester scholarships for 40+ underprivileged children of Indonesia.

Why Rebuild Eco-Schools?

BudayaBaik's eco-school initiative addresses critical challenges in Indonesia's education and environmental sectors. By rebuilding schools using recycled plastic materials, we tackle multiple issues simultaneously.

Our eco-schools provide safe, resilient learning environments while removing significant amounts of plastic waste from landfills and oceans. Each school we build represents a step toward a more sustainable and equitable future for Indonesia.

Recent Projects

Yayasan Sayap Ibu Hydrophonics Initiative

Hosted a hydrophonics building event at Yayasan Sayap Ibu. Teaching and bringing joy to 30+ underprivileged children and disabled orphans regarding photosynthesis and plant related topics. Building a hydrophonics system for the orphanage centre and raising IDR 500,000 to donate 100+ tomato and lettuce seeds.

Sekolah PAUD's Sustainable Transformation and Scholarship Program

Fundraised $30,000 USD to rebuild sekolah PAUD in Bogor, Indonesia. From learning in cramped, hot spaces in the principals personal home to a safe, and green furnished eco-block school. Provided educational toys, chairs and benches hosting eco-block volunteer programs and mural painting initiatives. Impacting 300+ community members and providing semester scholarships for 50+ underprivileged students.

Educational Material Donations

Donated IDR 1,500,000 in collaboration with Golden Rama Travels and Tours to provide recycling and hygiene infrastructure, educational materials (posters and books), stationary and educational toys. Hosted art painting events regarding "A sustainable environment and living eco-systems around us" and taught 40+ students environment related STEM lessons.
